What is Bellisseria?
Continent overview · the basics in plain language
▼

Bellisseria is a Linden-Lab-owned continent that hosts the second generation of Linden Homes — the homes included with paid memberships. It launched in 2019, has grown to over 2,800 regions, and is built and maintained by the Moles (a small team of long-time SL residents employed by Linden Lab).

Unlike older themed estates, Bellisseria blends themes across one connected continent: a Victorian neighbourhood might sit beside a houseboat dock, with a log-home valley a short railway ride away. The whole thing is navigable by boat, train, road, and air.

Who lives there
Plus, Premium, and Premium Plus members. Basic accounts cannot claim a Linden Home but are welcome to visit.
Parcel sizes
512 m² (Plus), 1,024 m² (Premium), and 2,048 m² (Premium Plus exclusive themes).
Use restrictions
Residential only — no commerce, rentals, or advertising (except in the new Ridgewood Enclave commercial districts).
Staying in theme
Parcels must be kept presentable and broadly in keeping with their neighbourhood's theme.

Linden Home themes
All current themes · tier eligibility · quick descriptions
▼
Tier guide: Plus members access 512 m² themes · Premium adds 1,024 m² themes · Premium Plus unlocks the larger 2,048 m² exclusive themes. Newer themes such as Copperfield Heights mix all three tiers in one community.
Traditional Premium
The original Bellisseria look — suburban houses with gardens, picket fences, a friendly neighbourhood feel. The continent's heart.
Houseboats Premium
Floating homes moored along Bellisseria's waterways. Boat access from the dock. Popular for water-themed roleplay and sailing.
Campers Plus
Compact 512 m² parcels with a vintage caravan. Wooded camping-ground feel. Entry-level option for Plus members.
Victorian Premium
Gaslit cobbles, ornate trim, parks with reflecting pools. Connected by an extensive railway network. Atmospheric at night.
Log Homes Premium
Upscale log cabins set in forested hills and valleys. Country living with modern interiors. Hiking and horse-riding country.
Stilt Homes Premium
Houses raised over water on stilts. Coastal feel, sea views, easy boat access. House controller is in the water.
Chalets Premium
Alpine-style cabins set among mountains and pine, with their own railway. Cosy interiors suited to winter scenery.
Fantasy Premium
Whimsical dwellings — treehouses, mushroom homes, magical themes. Includes the Fairstone Quarry wedding venue.
Newbrooke Plus Premium
Modern suburban homes with customisable siding, trim, and brickwork colours. Eight models across 512 and 1,024 m².
Sakura Plus Premium
Japanese-inspired homes among cherry blossoms and traditional gardens. Released for SL's 19th birthday in 2022.
Ranch Premium Plus
Large 2,048 m² parcels with ranch-style houses, paddocks, and rural landscapes. Horse country.
Mediterranean Premium Plus
Sun-drenched coastal villas with terracotta tiles and stucco walls. Has its own railway connecting the regions.
Thousand Island Premium Plus
Lakeside homes set across an archipelago of small islands. Quiet, water-focused theme for Premium Plus residents.
Aspen Ridge Premium
Mountain homes with seasonal landscape changes — the regions visibly shift through the seasons.
Ridgewood Enclave Premium Plus
Premium Plus theme that now also hosts Bellisseria's first commercial districts (March 2026) — the only place commerce is permitted on the continent.
Copperfield Heights All tiers
2026 release. Craftsman-style homes that mix Plus, Premium, and Premium Plus parcels in the same community for the first time.
First-generation themes (Tahoe, Elderglen, Sahreta Osumai, Meadowbrook) still exist on older estates but are no longer claimable. Some long-term residents still occupy them. They use older covenants and have a 175-prim limit on 512 m².

How to claim a Linden Home
Step-by-step · what to tell a newcomer asking
▼
Have an eligible membership Plus, Premium, or Premium Plus. Basic accounts cannot claim a Linden Home. Tier upgrade is done at secondlife.com → My Account → Membership.
Check land allowance A Linden Home counts against the resident's land allowance. If they already own mainland that uses their full tier, they need to free up some allowance first.
Go to the claim page secondlife.com → My Account → Get a Linden Home. Available themes are listed by tier, with home models, parcel sizes, and a region map.
Browse and pick a parcel Filter by theme. Click an available parcel to preview its surroundings on the map. Don't rush — neighbours and views matter and swapping is easy if it doesn't work out.
Confirm and teleport Once claimed, the resident gets a teleport landmark and a House Controller appears just outside the parcel boundary (or in the water, for stilt homes).
Furnishing & customising The House Controller (touch it on the parcel) controls textures, accessories, and reset. Furniture is up to the resident — Bellisseria covenants forbid replacing the house structure itself.
Premium Plus tip: Premium Plus members can request a specific Linden Home via a support ticket if a particular parcel becomes available. They also get exclusive access to Ranch, Mediterranean, Thousand Island, and Ridgewood Enclave themes.

Abandoning & swapping: About Land → General → Abandon Land releases a Linden Home back to the pool. The resident can claim a different one immediately — there's no cooldown.

Getting around
Trains, boats, roads, balloons · how the continent connects
▼

One of Bellisseria's defining features is that it's fully traversable. Residents can travel from any region to any other without teleporting — by rail, water, road, or air. Several rezzing zones are scattered across the continent for vehicles.

🚂
Railway network
Continent-spanning rail system with separate networks for Victorian, Chalet, and Mediterranean regions. Trains rez at marked stations — touch a station sign to ride.
⛵
Waterways
Bellisseria is built around navigable canals, rivers, and ocean. Boat rezzers at marina parcels and community docks. Houseboats and stilt homes have private rezzers.
🚗
Roads
A road network connects neighbourhoods. Vehicle rezzing zones are placed at community hubs — touch the rez board or use a rezzer parcel.
🎈
Air & balloons
Hot-air balloon rezzers exist at several spots for slow scenic tours. Aircraft are permitted but be mindful of altitude limits and neighbouring parcels.
🚲
Bicycles & horses
Free bicycle rezzers at many community areas. Some Linden Home residents keep horses (or dragons, or moths) and ride them between parcels.
📍
Rezzing zones
Marked public rezzing zones let any resident temporarily rez a vehicle on otherwise-restricted parcels. Look for the signposts at major hubs.
Note for newcomers: Vehicles can be tricky for new residents — sim crossings still occasionally cause hiccups. If a resident is struggling, suggest a teleport for now and an exploration trip once they're more comfortable with the viewer.
