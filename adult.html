Age & Maturity

Getting Access

Before you can explore adult Second Life you need two things: age verification on your Linden Lab account, and the correct maturity settings in your viewer.

Age Requirement

Second Life requires all users to be 18 or older to access adult content. Age is verified at account creation. If you haven't verified yet, visit accounts.secondlife.com.

Firestorm Viewer
01
Open Preferences

Top menu: Avatar › Preferences

02
General Tab

Click the General tab in the left column.

03
Enable All Ratings

Under Maturity Rating tick General, Moderate, and Adult.

04
Quick Bar

You can also click the G / M / A icons in the top toolbar to toggle ratings instantly.

Official SL Viewer
01
Open Preferences

Top menu: Me › Preferences

02
General Tab

Click the General tab.

03
Select Adult

Under Maturity ratings I want to see select Adult — this also enables General and Moderate automatically.

04
Click OK

Takes effect immediately.

What to Expect on Arrival

Most adult regions show an age-gate door — click and confirm 18+. Some require joining a group for full access. Explicit content will be visible; that's normal. Read the posted rules before engaging. You can always mute sounds in Preferences if gestures become overwhelming.

Sex Furniture

How It Works

Sex furniture is any in-world object containing animations for adult poses. Beds, sofas, bondage equipment, wall mounts, portable pose balls — the object drives the animation, you just sit.

01
Sit on it

Right-click the furniture and select Sit, or simply touch it. Some objects use a menu ball nearby.

02
Choose a pose

A dialog menu lists available poses or activities. Select one — your avatar moves into position automatically.

03
Invite others (optional)

Multi-avatar furniture will prompt nearby people to join. They must accept — nobody is force-sat.

04
Adjust position

Most furniture has X/Y/Z nudge buttons in the menu so you can align your specific body shape. Some save your settings.

05
Stand up to exit

Press Page Up or click Stand. You're always free to leave any animation at any time.

Animation Engines
XTAC / XTC

Widely used, clean menus, good sync. The most common engine you'll encounter.

RLV / RLVA

Advanced engine enabling automatic body interaction and restraint play. Requires RLV enabled in your viewer.

Bento-Native

Newer furniture that animates finger, toe and facial Bento bones for expressive, natural movement.

Xcite

Interaction specific to Xcite body parts. Adds touch, arousal and response systems.

Buying Checklist

Look for: Bento support · pose count (20+ is good) · body compatibility list · land impact (LI) · copy/modify permissions for adjustment · demo room available.

Consent & Safety

Your Safety Matters

Consent is required for everything in Second Life. Always ask first. If someone says no — or doesn't respond — that is a no.

🛡️
Built-in SL Protections

You must click to sit on anything. Objects must request permission to animate you. Scripts must request permission to attach items. You can always stand up.

🔒
RLV — Your Choice

Restrained Love Viewer is off by default. Enable it only if you trust your partner fully. In Firestorm: Avatar › Preferences › Firestorm › RLV.

🔴
Safe Words

Agree a safe word before any restricted-play scene. "Red" or "Safeword" are universally understood. A good partner will always give you one first.

🚫
Block & Report

Right-click any avatar › Block to stop all contact. Use Help › Report Abuse for ToS violations. Report rule-breaking to region moderators.

Personal Safety: Never share real-world personal information in SL. Use payment methods with strong fraud protection. Be cautious of anyone who rushes intimacy or applies pressure. You are always free to log off — no explanation needed.
The Golden Rule

Consent is not just a rule — it's the culture that makes adult SL work. Communities that thrive are communities where members look after each other. Be the kind of resident you'd want to meet on your first day.
