✨ What is this place?

Second Life is a 3D virtual world — not a game. There are no levels, no quests, no goals. You won't win anything. You decide what to do.

Explore, make friends, go to live music or club nights, dance, build, shop, create clothes or art, roleplay, learn a language, fall in love — thousands of different communities run their own corner of SL. If you're not sure where to start, pick a place below and just go. You can always teleport home with Ctrl+Shift+H.

What is a Mesh Body?
Think of the default avatar as a basic mannequin. A mesh body is a custom suit you wear over it — much better detail and compatible with modern clothing. They cost L$ but free demos let you try before you buy.
Female: Maitreya LaraX · eBODY Reborn · Legacy
Male: Legacy Athletic · Belleza Jake · Signature Gianni X
Heads: LeLutka (most popular — free demos in-store)

🔀 My avatar looks wrong — what do I do?
My avatar is naked, grey, or missing a body part / clothing item
↓
Is your whole avatar naked or grey?
Yes — naked or grey
Go to Avatar → Avatar Welcome Pack in the top menu bar and choose a complete starter look — it's free and instant
↓
Still grey / cloudy?
↓
Go to Avatar → Avatar Health → Rebake Textures
Shortcut: Ctrl+Alt+R (PC) · Cmd+Option+R (Mac)
↓
Still not fixed? Change to a different region or relog
↓
✓ Fixed!
No — just a missing piece
Open Inventory (Ctrl+I) and click the WORN tab
↓
Note the folder name shown next to your body or outfit — e.g. "Female body - B" or "Female look - Casual"
↓
In main inventory go to Clothing → [that folder name]. Items in bold are worn — the item that's not bold is the missing piece
↓
Right-click the missing item → Add
↓
✓ Fixed!
Everyone has disappeared — including me!
You've accidentally toggled avatar rendering off. Press Ctrl+Alt+Shift+4 (PC) or Cmd+Option+Shift+4 (Mac) to bring everyone back
Something is stuck to my head (or body) and won't come off!
Right-click the attachment → Detach. Or right-click your avatar → Take Off → Detach All to remove everything at once.
↓
Still stuck?
↓
It's a ghost attachment — the viewer thinks it's gone but your screen hasn't caught up. Log out and log back in. That always clears it.
↓
✓ Gone!

Someone is bothering me — what do I do?
Simple steps to stay safe and take back control
🚫
1. Block them
Right-click the avatar → Block. You won't see or hear them again on your viewer. Blocking is private — they aren't told.
🏠
2. Teleport home
Press Ctrl+Shift+H (PC) or Cmd+Shift+H (Mac) to teleport home instantly. If you haven't set a home yet, teleport to any safe place from the World Map.
📝
3. File an Abuse Report
If someone breaks the rules, report them to Linden Lab. Go to Help → Report Abuse, pick a category, describe what happened and submit. Take a screenshot first with Ctrl+Shift+S.
🛑
4. Find a Mentor
Look for anyone wearing a Mentor tag — they're volunteers trained to help. You can also return to the Desktop Learning Center or Welcome Hub where mentors are often nearby.
🔒 Stop people pushing or shoving you

Agalmic Movelock — free (L$0)

A small HUD you wear. It softly locks your avatar in place when you're standing still, so nobody can push you around. The moment you move, it releases — no jitter, no snap-back. Auto-off when you sit, auto-on when you stand. Includes Find Space, which automatically moves you to an empty spot when you teleport into a crowd. Works in every viewer.

Adult Content in Second Life
What you need to know before exploring this side of SL
🔞
You Must Be 18 or Over
Adult content in Second Life is strictly for residents aged 18 and over. This is enforced by Linden Lab's Terms of Service — misrepresenting your age is a violation.
⚙️
It's Opt-In
Adult content is not available by default. Enable it in Preferences → General and set your content access to General, Moderate, Adult.
📍
Adult Regions Only
Adult content must take place on Adult-rated regions only — it is not permitted on General or Moderate land. Adult regions are clearly labelled in the viewer.
🤝
Consent & Respect
Always ask before initiating any intimate interaction — and anyone can withdraw consent at any time. Start with conversation, not action. Remember: behind every avatar is a real person with real feelings.
🏠 The Adult Hub

The Adult Hub is a welcoming, tasteful space created by Linden Lab for residents who are 18 or over and curious about the adult side of Second Life. It's not a venue full of strangers — think of it as a well-designed adult social area with beautiful landscaping where you can find your bearings at your own pace. The Hub includes private hotel rooms (available for 30 minutes with a partner or partners), saunas, a pool, a naturist beach, a main beach, and a club. You'll also find information boards, teleport portals to adult locations and entertainment across the grid, and the ThundrGO dating HUD. The Hub itself is calm and social — well moderated, with everyone clothed on the day of writing. When you arrive you'll be asked to agree to the region rules and confirm your maturity rating is set to Adult. Adult-themed furniture throughout the Hub includes built-in consent systems — you'll be asked to accept any interaction before it begins.
